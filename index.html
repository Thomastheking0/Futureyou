Our Approach

The work is in
the adapting.

A restaurant is not a machine you can tune once and forget. It is a living system — breathing with its team, its guests, its neighbourhood, and the shifting realities of the world around it.

Most operators don't struggle because they lack passion. They struggle because what they are carrying has become more complex than any one person can manage without support and structure.

Improvement, therefore, is not an event. It is a continuous process.

Our work begins with clarity. A rigorous diagnostic reveals where value is being lost — in operations, in experience, in trust — and what must change first. But insight alone doesn't change a business. Action does. The operators who build lasting restaurants adapt quickly, decide on evidence, and adjust without ego when the data points in a new direction.

Our role is to give you a sharper view and a structured path forward. Yours is to lead the change.

Common Questions

Honest answers, up front.

What's the difference between the two services?
The Operational Diagnostic is a self-assessment of how the restaurant runs — your menu, financials, operations, people, and strategy. The Web & Social Media Analysis is an expert review of how the world sees you online — your website, Google profile, reviews, and social channels. Most operators benefit from both, which is why the bundle exists.
What does the 199 EUR include?
The full online diagnostic, your overall health score, your domain-by-domain breakdown, your ranked diagnosis, your prioritized action plan, an instant PDF report you keep, and an optional 15-minute follow-up call to walk through anything that's unclear. No hidden fees, no upsells.
How long does the diagnostic take?
About 30 minutes if you don't overthink it. Fifty short questions on a 1-to-5 agreement scale. Go with your gut — your gut is usually right, and we'll surface the gaps either way. You can pause and resume; it's fully online.
Is the follow-up call really optional?
Yes. The report is designed to stand on its own — most operators read it, mark the actions they want to start, and get going. The 15-minute call is included if you want to talk it through with someone, but you don't have to take it. No pressure either way.
Is everything confidential?
Yes. Everything you share with us — your numbers, your responses, anything discussed on the follow-up call — is fully confidential. We don't share, sell, or publish anything about your restaurant. Your report is for you alone.
Does this work for quick-service or just full-service?
Both. The framework is calibrated for independent operators across formats — full-service, fast-casual, café, and quick-service. The labor and food cost benchmarks adjust automatically. It's not built for chains over 10 locations.
What if my numbers are bad and I'm embarrassed?
Most operators we talk to are running a business with 3–5 serious leaks they could fix this quarter. That's not a moral failing — that's the industry. The whole point of the diagnostic is to find the leaks before they sink the boat. Your answers stay between you and us.
