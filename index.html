Common Questions

Honest answers, up front.

What does the 12,000 SEK include?
A short preparation call, the 30-minute guided diagnostic session itself, the written report delivered as a PDF within 48 hours, and a 30-day follow-up call to check progress on your action plan. No hidden fees and no upsell — one engagement, one price.
How long does the session take?
About 30 minutes if you don't overthink it. We work through fifty short questions on a 1-to-5 agreement scale together. Go with your gut — your gut is usually right, and we'll surface the gaps either way.
Is the conversation confidential?
Yes. Everything you share with us — your numbers, your concerns, your responses — is fully confidential. We don't share, sell, or publish anything about your restaurant. Your written report is for you alone.
Does this work for quick-service or just full-service?
Both. The framework is calibrated for independent operators across formats — full-service, fast-casual, café, and quick-service. The labor and food cost benchmarks adjust automatically. It's not built for chains over 10 locations.
What if my numbers are bad and I'm embarrassed?
Most operators we talk to are running a business with 3–5 serious leaks they could fix this quarter. That's not a moral failing — that's the industry. The whole point of the engagement is to find the leaks before they sink the boat. Your answers stay between you and your advisor.
